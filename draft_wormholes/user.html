Joonas Rikkonen

Creator

Joonas Rikkonen (commonly known as Regalis) is the creator and the main coder for SCP - Containment Breach as well as the creator ofSCP-087-B. Besides taking care of the development of the code for the game, Regalis is also involved in graphic design, sound, and 3D modelling.
